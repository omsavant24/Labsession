Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a legendary warrior king and the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India. Known for his valor, strategic warfare, and visionary leadership, he inspired countless people through his life of justice, bravery, and devotion to Swarajya.

Why I Admire Him

  • He established a strong and progressive kingdom based on justice and welfare.
  • He was a master of guerrilla warfare and a brilliant military tactician.
  • He respected women and promoted secular values in his administration.
